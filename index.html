About ACA Rwanda

A New Approach to Education

Accelerated Christian Academy (ACA) is strategically established in Matimba, within the Nyagatare District of Rwanda. Situated in Rwanda's northeastern extremity, Nyagatare District is the largest and one of the fastest-growing regions.

ACA addresses existing educational gaps by implementing a distinctive, innovative, and holistic educational model. Central to ACA's mission is a commitment to fostering social and economic progress within the region, driven by quality education grounded in strong moral values.

Under the visionary leadership of founder Sylvia Katushabe, ACA has emerged as a center of academic excellence and ethical growth, supported by an experienced Board of Governors.

Our Education Model

Self-Paced Learning

Each student progresses at their own pace, allowing them to master subjects thoroughly before advancing.

Biblically Integrated Curriculum

The curriculum combines academic rigor with Christian values, fostering intellectual growth alongside moral and spiritual development.

Competency-Based Advancement

Students advance based on their ability to demonstrate mastery in each subject area, accommodating different learning speeds.

Critical Thinking & Problem-Solving

Our framework nurtures analytical thinking, practical problem-solving skills, and independent learning habits.

Character Development

We emphasize values such as discipline, responsibility, and leadership, equipping students with ethical decision-making skills.

Digital Learning Integration

Leveraging digital tools to create flexible, inclusive, and efficient learning experiences for all students.

Our Impact

Community Impact

ACA has introduced a structured and secure learning environment, mitigating past security concerns. Students can now attend school without fear, allowing them to fully engage in their studies.

Our initiatives have encouraged greater involvement from parents, local leaders, and stakeholders who now recognize the long-term advantages of investing in quality education.

Regional Impact

The positive effects of ACA extend beyond Matimba, influencing the broader Nyagatare District and neighboring regions. By offering a high standard of education, ACA is setting a benchmark for other schools in the area.

The success of ACA serves as a model for similar initiatives in other parts of Rwanda, particularly in regions that face similar challenges in education.

Future Expansion

ACA prioritizes the expansion of its educational reach to underserved communities, ensuring that students from diverse backgrounds benefit from its specialized learning model.

We are committed to leveraging digital tools to create flexible, inclusive, and efficient learning experiences through online platforms and hybrid education models.
